Enable Dark Mode in FractionCalc

Available starting from Beta v1.0.6 and later versions.

Step 1: Open the Ribbon Menu

In the FractionCalc app, go to the top menu and click View.
On Android version, open Settings > Adjust Mode > Choose Dark Mode.

Step 2: Toggle Dark Mode

Select Toggle Dark Mode. The app will instantly switch to a sleek, eye-friendly dark theme.

Tip: You can switch between Dark and Light Mode anytime without restarting the app.
