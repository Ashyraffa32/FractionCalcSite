Change the Background

Customize FractionCalc by setting your own photo as the background.
Available in Version 1.2 and later.

Step 1: Open the Ribbon

Click the View option in the Ribbon at the top of the app window, then select Change Background.

Step 2: Select Your Photo

Choose a photo from your computer and click Open. FractionCalc will instantly update the background.

Note: The appearance of the file selection dialog may vary depending on your operating system.
