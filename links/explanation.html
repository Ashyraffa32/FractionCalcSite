Show Explanations

Understand more aboout each fractions you're calculating.
Available in Version 1.5.0 and later.

Step 1: Hit the "Show Explanation" checkbox

Under the calculation area, hit the "Show Explanation" checkbox first before calculating.

Step 2: Calculate like usual.

Explanations for your calculation will appear automatically as long the option is turned on.
